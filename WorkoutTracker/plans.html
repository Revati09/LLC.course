Workout Plans

Beginner Plan

Beginner

Perfect for those starting their fitness journey. Focus on building strength and proper form.

Weekly Schedule:

  • Monday: Upper Body (Push)
  • Tuesday: Lower Body
  • Wednesday: Rest or Light Cardio
  • Thursday: Upper Body (Pull)
  • Friday: Full Body
  • Weekend: Rest

Sample Exercises:

  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Dumbbell Rows
  • Planks

Intermediate Plan

Intermediate

For those with some experience. Increase intensity and introduce compound movements.

Weekly Schedule:

  • Monday: Chest & Triceps
  • Tuesday: Back & Biceps
  • Wednesday: Legs
  • Thursday: Shoulders & Abs
  • Friday: Full Body Circuit
  • Weekend: Active Recovery

Sample Exercises:

  • Bench Press
  • Deadlifts
  • Overhead Press
  • Pull-ups

Advanced Plan

Advanced

High-intensity training for experienced lifters. Focus on progressive overload and specialization.

Weekly Schedule:

  • Monday: Heavy Chest
  • Tuesday: Heavy Back
  • Wednesday: Heavy Legs
  • Thursday: Light Upper Body
  • Friday: Heavy Shoulders
  • Saturday: Conditioning
  • Sunday: Rest

Sample Exercises:

  • Squats (Heavy)
  • Bench Press (Heavy)
  • Deadlifts
  • Olympic Lifts
